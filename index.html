About

Full-Stack Web Developer with past experience in team-oriented environments acquired through work on independent film production. I have since gained a certificate in Full-Stack Web Development from the University of Texas at Austin where I learned and built upon skills in JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS.

One of my most recent group projects, Myooz, enabled users to find people to go to upcoming music events with. With teamwork and creativity I was able to produce a responsive and creative design for the front-end. I also used my knowledge of MySQL to store users and the groups they’ve created in a database.

I am eager to offer my technical and collaborative skills to continue creating quality web applications.
